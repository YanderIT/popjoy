## Processing Time

All orders ship within **5 business days** under normal circumstances. During peak seasons or promotional events, processing times may be slightly extended. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

We offer **free worldwide shipping** to non-remote areas. Our shipping partners cover most major cities and regions globally.

### Remote Areas

For customers located in remote or hard-to-reach areas, including **American Outer Islands**, additional shipping fees may apply. Our team will contact you before processing your order if additional charges are necessary.

Estimated delivery time: **7-15 days** worldwide.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs processing, local postal services, or unforeseen circumstances.

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package through our shipping partner's website.

## Shipping Issues
